#### Research

My interests sit at the intersection of **classical machine learning**, **signal processing**, and **model interpretability** — from matrix factorization and hypothesis testing to explanation methods such as SHAP, and linear-algebraic approaches built from first principles.

#### Current work

I am developing **reactive marimo notebooks** for matrix decomposition (SVD, PCA, QR), **medical ML pipelines** with interpretability at each stage, and **LangChain-based data agents** for exploratory analysis workflows.
